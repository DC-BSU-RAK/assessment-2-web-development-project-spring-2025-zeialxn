About Us

Beary Bites is a homemade mini donuts business founded
by Thatiana, Irish, and Zhealene. What started as a shared
passion turned into a small business dedicated to creating
fress, high-quality mini donuts made from scratch. At Beary
Bites, we focus on delivering delicious, bite-sized treats
that are perfect for sharing, gifting, or simply treating yourself.
